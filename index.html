Filipino Dishes that I like

Pork Adobo

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are many different types of meat you can use to make adobo but I prefer Pork.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Sinigang

Sinigang na baboy is made up of pork ribs, different types of vegetables, and tamarind flavored broth. It tastes good and is one of my favorite dish.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai is a Filipino-style spring roll made up of ground pork, chicken or beef filling wrapped in thin crepes called Lumpia wrappers. The meat-stuffed lumpia is rolled into a thin cylindrical shape, cut into bite-size lengths and deep-fried to perfection. When going to Filipino parties, you should expect the dish to be on the tables.
image source: Kitchen Confidante

Pork Sisig

Is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or an appetizer. The composition of the dish is mainly of pig's parts such as minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili peppers are also added. I started to like Sisig because of that one time where I ate one.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Kare Kare

It is a Filipino stew/ curry complemented with a thick savory peanut sauce. It is made from a variaton base of stewed oxtail, beef tripe, pork hocks, calves feet, pig's feet or trotters, various cut of porks, beef stew meat, and occasionally oxtripe. It's earthy, slightly sweet, rich, yet mild-tasting.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Liempo

Inihaw na Liempo or grilled pork is marinated and grilled, basting meat as it cooks. Depending on the desiredtaste, a typical liempo marinade can be a mixture of any of the following: soy sauce, fish sauce, banana ketchup, garlic, kalamansi, brown sugar, salt, and or pepper.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Chicken Inasal

It is a grilled chicken dish and one of the well known Ilonggo dishes. This is done by marinating chicken pieces in a unique blend of spices and grilling them until done. The taste will depend on how you marinate the chicken and what you will use to marinate the chicken.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Bulalo

A beef soup comprised of shank with bone marrow still inside the bone. Bulalo is considered to be one of the most favorite main dish in the Philippines. Because of the popularity of the food, restaurants and eateries specializing in cooking Bulalo were put-up. Some of the famous "Bulalohan" can be found in Tagaytay City and Sto. Tomas Batangas.
image source: Panlasang Pinoy

Chicken Arroz Caldo

Chicken Arroz Caldo is a type of rice porridge. It is a popular dish here in the Philippines, it is usually eaten for breakfast or as a "meryenda". It is one of my favorite foods to eat during a cold weather
